# Marketing Hero Archetype

Use for landing pages, campaign pages, splash sections, and product introductions where first impression matters most.

## Best System Fits

- `glass`
- `neo-brutalism`
- `neo-m3`
- `minimal`
- `swiss`
- `skeuomorphism` for highly intentional niche themes

## Core Regions

- announcement or trust strip
- dominant headline and supporting copy
- primary and secondary CTA
- visual proof area: product shot, illustration, graphic, or media
- credibility block: logos, metrics, testimonial, or feature callouts

## Behavior Rules

- Establish one clear visual thesis
- Keep CTA hierarchy obvious
- Avoid generic gradient sludge and interchangeable SaaS hero layouts
- Support mobile first, then widen the composition

## Good Hybrids

- `ant` + `glass` when a product page needs stronger brand atmosphere but consistent product language
- `minimal` + `neo-m3` for premium editorial tech launches

## Starter Structure

```text
Announcement Strip
Hero Copy and CTAs
Visual Proof Area
Credibility Block
```
