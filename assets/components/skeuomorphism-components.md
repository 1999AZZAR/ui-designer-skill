# Skeuomorphism Components

Use these patterns when the product intentionally mimics physical materials or nostalgic interfaces.

## Component Priorities

- Primary: leather button, glossy button, dial, switch, metal toolbar, wood panel, embossed card
- Supporting: reflections, highlights, engraved labels, recessed fields

## Behavior Rules

- Commit to a coherent material story
- Use texture and depth intentionally, not everywhere
- Keep interaction targets clear despite ornament
- Reserve for creative, luxury, nostalgic, or game-like surfaces

## Material Mapping

- Leather: premium actions, notebooks, dark control surfaces
- Metal: utility bars, knobs, audio or system controls
- Wood: panels, frames, decorative containers
- Glass/gloss: highlights, call-to-action emphasis

## Example Pattern

- Vintage control panel
  - brushed metal toolbar
  - leather primary controls
  - recessed status display
  - wood-framed content surface
