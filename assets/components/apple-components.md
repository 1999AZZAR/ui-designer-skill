# Apple Components

Use these patterns for interfaces that should feel native to iOS or macOS.

## Page Scaffold

- Clear top navigation or large-title header
- Spacious content sections
- High emphasis on motion, hierarchy, and material layering

## Component Priorities

- Primary: segmented control, sheet, card, list row, toolbar button, tab bar
- Supporting: blur surfaces, contextual menus, inline feedback, toggles

## Behavior Rules

- Respect platform navigation conventions
- Use depth and vibrancy sparingly but intentionally
- Maintain generous touch targets and calm spacing
- Motion should feel smooth and physically consistent

## Tailwind Notes

- Use soft radii and blur layers
- Keep typography clean and platform-like
- Avoid overly heavy borders

## Example Pattern

- Settings or content app
  - large title
  - grouped cards or inset list sections
  - bottom tab bar or top toolbar
