# Playful Genre

Post-Linear soft school. For consumer apps, casual, community, onboarding.

## Character
- Rounded sans display, warm accent
- Generous spacing, soft shadows
- Playful microinteractions (spring, bounce)

## Theme Cluster
Hum, Carnival

## Slop Gate Overrides
- Playful loosens: gate 33 (no bounce) — spring easing allowed on non-functional elements
- Playful loosens: gate 5 (emoji in UI) — emoji allowed in body copy, not in nav/actions
- Playful tightens: gate 7 (CTA voice) — friendly but specific
- Playful skips: gate 4 (re-drawn chrome) — does not apply; chrome can be part of the playful aesthetic

## Voice Fixtures
- CTA: friendly, encouraging (try it free, join the fun)
- Headlines: conversational, benefit-led
- Labels: warm, human
