# Atmospheric Genre

Suno / Runway / dark-AI-tool school. For generative AI, music, video, late-night apps.

## Character
- Dark paper (L < 15%), high-chroma accent
- Bold display type, often mono or grotesk
- Immersive, layered surfaces with glass effects
